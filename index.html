    I am Rafael Luis Evangelista, a dedicated Game Developer with a passion for creating innovative and engaging gameplay experiences. A recent Cum Laude graduate from Ateneo de Manila University, I hold degrees in Computer Science and Digital Game Design & Development. My academic journey, combined with hands-on experience in game development, has equipped me with strong proficiency in C++, C#, and Python, alongside a deep understanding of game design principles. I thrive in collaborative environments and have successfully led projects that blend creativity and technical expertise.
    Beyond my technical skills, I am deeply involved in extracurricular activities, having taken on leadership roles such as Vice President of Finance for the Contemporary A cappella Singers Association of the Ateneo. These experiences have honed my communication, teamwork, and organizational abilities, which I apply to every project I undertake. I approach game development with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that each element aligns with the broader vision while maintaining high-quality standards. My portfolio highlights my dedication to crafting immersive and educational experiences, from advanced AI-driven gameplay mechanics to incorporating cultural narratives like Philippine mythology, reflecting my passion for both technical innovation and creative storytelling.
